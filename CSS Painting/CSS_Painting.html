This is a replica of Piet Mondrian’s “Tableau No. IV; Lozenge Composition with Red, Gray, Blue, Yellow, and Black.” This painting was created in 1924 and features blocks of primary color, separated by thick black lines, on top of a grey background. This is the signature of Mondrian, as he only works with straight lines and boxes of primary colors. Though coding I have replicated his painting as closely as possible. Of course this has a much smoother appearance to it, considering the original was made with oil paint.
